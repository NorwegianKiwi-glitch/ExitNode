Why ExitNode?

Imagine a fire breaks out in an apartment building. The alarm blares. Residents try to escape—some are disoriented, others in shock. The scene is chaotic, with crowds of people rushing out.

When lives are at stake and time is critical, registration and further handling must happen quickly and accurately. In a situation where people are in varying conditions, it is essential that as many as possible can register themselves—without placing additional strain on already overburdened first responders.

Our Solution

Our product is a digital registration solution for evacuees. Individuals can easily register themselves using a code token or biometrics and receive a unique ID linked to an ID wristband. When the wristband is scanned at a new location, the system updates their information—providing a real-time overview of the person’s last known registration point.

This frees up first responders and gives the incident commander better control, while also reducing errors and providing a more reliable foundation for decisions in a chaotic situation.

This isn’t just about technology. It’s about safety, precision—and saving lives.
